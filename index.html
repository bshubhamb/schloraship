Druhin Foundation is to commemorate the life and journey of DRUHIN SUKLABAIDYA (5 th March 1972 – 17 th August 2021) who is remembered for his compassion, boundless energy and his spirit of living in the moment. He may not be known among all like a celebrity but those who knew him or crossed paths with him, find it difficult to forget him and, remember him with great fondness. He was someone courageous or maybe foolish enough to take the untrodden path and create his own journey. He was - bundle of energy, always on some mission; passionate about his causes, be it politics or work; ethical and principled with his own sets of ethics which he did not cross no matter what; compassionate which is a rare quality in present time; hopeful and optimistic about the future which made him teach and motivate young people; the one with an endless thirst for knowledge (he was Google, pre – Google days); a lovable son, motivating and warm brother, dependable and loyal friend; a generous and lovable father and a faithful devotee of his guru.
